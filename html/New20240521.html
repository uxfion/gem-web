Macao Polytechnic University research team wins ICME Ground Challenge 2024

May  21  2024 , Tue

A research team from Macao Polytechnic University (MPU) has won the ICME Ground Challenge 2024! They won the first place in Track 5 (Video Guidance) and Track 6 (Animal Behavior Recognition), demonstrating their excellence in multimodal video reasoning and analysis. We are honored to collaborate with the world's top hospitals and universities to advance scientific and clinical development in the field of breast cancer. Our partners include Radboud umc, Macau Polytechnic University, Netherlands Cancer Institute, Case Western Reserve University, EKFZ for Digital Health, University of Pittsburgh, Universitat de Girona, Maastricht University，Uniklinik Rwthaachen，Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center.

The symposium will provide an opportunity for breast cancer researchers, clinicians and technical experts from around the world to exchange and share the latest research results, innovative technologies and clinical practices.
