Recently, led by Macao Polytechnic University, the "Fetal ultrasound intelligent quality control standards
Published Tue 21Nov 2023
Recently, led by Macao Polytechnic University, the "Fetal ultrasound intelligent quality control standards, key technologies and demonstration application research" project jointly declared by RayShape Medical and Shenzhen University was officially approved by the Macao Science and Technology Development Fund (Science and Technology Fund) and the Guangdong Science and Technology Department to jointly carry out joint scientific research funding project plan.

Centering on the deployment of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area planning and construction of an international science and technology innovation center, the project aims to solve the problems of intelligent quality control standards, key technologies and demonstration applications faced by fetal ultrasound images, and will bring new development opportunities and hopes for the development of fetal ultrasound images. The project will be phased in over the next three years.

"The approval of this project is very significant for us as it will bring us higher quality clinical fetal ultrasound images and create more opportunities for development in this field." Relevant person in charge said. We are excited and excited about the approval of this project and believe that it will bring positive changes and developments in the field of medical image processing.
