Ruijin Hospital of Shanghai Jiaotong University School of Medicine Wins Third Prize in Generative AI Track of "2023 DiMTAIC" Competition

Dec  16  2023 , Sat

Recently, the final round of "Digital Medical Technology and Application Innovation Competition 2023 (DiMTAIC 2023)" co-sponsored by Ruijin Hospital affiliated to Shanghai Jiaotong University School of Medicine and Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory came to an end successfully and the results were announced. Our team won the third prize in Track 2: "Medical Human-Computer Interaction Application Based on Generative AI Technology", with project number B67, which is called "One Question, Two Answers" Medical Diagnostic Language Model for Integrative Chinese and Western Medicine.

This honor signifies that our team's excellent contribution and innovative achievements in the field of digital medical technology have been highly recognized. By leading the way in generative AI technology, the project "One Question, Two Answers" successfully integrates Chinese and Western medical practices to build an advanced medical human-computer interaction application. This model has set a new benchmark for the development of medical diagnostic language combining Chinese and Western medicine.

"We are deeply honored to win the third prize in the DiMTAIC 2023 competition, which is the result of the hard work of the whole team." The team leader said that the project "One Question, Two Answers" is dedicated to promoting the deep integration of the medical field with artificial intelligence, providing doctors with a smarter and more efficient diagnostic tool. This award encourages us to continue on the path of technological innovation and expand new possibilities for future cooperation between medicine and artificial intelligence.
